Of Fish & Chips: Alpha Atlantis

Atlantis has been taken over by a men's right group and it's up to you to liberate it. This game is about redemption and liberation. It's also about mending a broken relationship and trying to save the world from sinister threats. Are you bad enough to play it?

Features of this game:

  • Multiple bullet types!
  • Lots of bosses!
  • Original scores. Including a song that features a Japanese voice synthesizer - something uncalled for.

Before playing the game, here are some known issues and points:

  • Audio won't play in Firefox. IE is ironically fine.
  • The audio may randomly cut off. However, it will eventually return.
  • The game's area is 800 x 600 pixels. Make sure your screen is large enough!
  • Change the bullet type by clicking buttons near the pause button.

Once your are aware of these points, then it's time to:

Play!

Tools

  • Framework: HTML5 + CreateJS
  • Editor: Notepad++
  • Music Composition: Anvil Studio + LMMS + Cevio Studio Free
  • Debugging: Internet Explorer + Chrome + Firefox
